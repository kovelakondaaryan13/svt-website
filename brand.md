# brand.md — Svayantra Tech Brand System

## Identity
- Company: Svayantra Tech
- Tagline: The Self-Operating Machine
- Sanskrit meaning: self-operating machine
- Positioning: AI-native automation production house for Indian MSMEs and factories

## Colors
- Navy:     #0F2D6B — primary brand, headers, nav, footer
- Cyan:     #38B6D8 — accents, highlights, CTAs
- Orange:   #F97316 — energy, secondary CTAs
- Gold:     #FCD34D — emphasis, badges
- White:    #FFFFFF — backgrounds, cards
- Off-white:#e9ebea — section alternates
- Gray:     #6B7280 — body text secondary
- Border:   #E5E7EB — card and input borders

## Typography
- Display / headings: Bebas Neue (Google Fonts)
- Body: Inter (Google Fonts)
- H1: 52px · H2: 36px · H3: 24px · H4: 18px
- Body: 16px · Small: 14px · Label: 12px
- Line height: 1.6 body · 1.2 headings
- Letter spacing: 0.02em headings

## Tone
- Warm and direct — not corporate
- India-first — references Indian MSMEs, factories, founders
- Founder-led — real, honest, no buzzwords
- Numbers always lead — real results, not adjectives
- Short sentences — punchy, never flowery

## Component Rules
- Primary button: #0F2D6B fill · white text · 8px radius · 16px 28px padding
- Secondary button: transparent · #0F2D6B border · #0F2D6B text
- Cards: white bg · 1px solid #E5E7EB · 12px radius · 24px padding
- Section padding: 80px vertical desktop · 48px mobile
- Max content width: 1100px centered
- Card shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.06)
- No gradients · No heavy drop shadows · No decorative noise

## Logo
- File: /logo.png
- Use on white or navy backgrounds only
- Never stretch or recolor
