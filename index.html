The SVT Approach

We don't replace what's already there. We connect everything — Tally, WhatsApp, Excel, your existing ERP — into one unified data layer. Then we add intelligence on top.

No migration. No disruption. Value in weeks, not months.

  • Enter — We walk your floor, map every process, and find the gaps where intelligence is missing.
  • Embed — We plug into your existing tools and deploy AI agents that handle decisions in real time.
  • Become — The system accumulates context, learns your patterns, and starts running itself.
